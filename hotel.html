Top 5-Star Hotels in Singapore

Goodwood Park Hotel

Located near Orchard Road, this heritage hotel provides a peaceful retreat with lush gardens, two outdoor pools, a fitness center, and several renowned restaurants. It’s perfect for those looking to relax and enjoy good food.

Approximate price: $200 per night

Booking: Goodwood Park Hotel

The St. Regis Singapore

Situated at the outskirts of Orchard Road, this luxurious hotel features elegantly decorated rooms, a fitness center, and a range of dining options. It’s a great choice for a regal stay without breaking the bank.

Approximate price: $310 per night (exclusive deals on Klook)

Booking: St. Regis Singapore on Klook

Andaz Singapore

Located in the vibrant Bugis area, this hotel offers stunning views, stylish interiors, and a variety of dining options. While service may vary, the overall experience and quality of food make it a worthwhile stay.

Approximate price: $200 per night

Booking: Andaz Singapore

Village Hotel Sentosa

Perfect for a family-friendly staycation, this hotel features four outdoor pools, kids' activities, and spacious rooms. It’s conveniently located near attractions like Adventure Cove Waterpark.

Approximate price: $200 per night

Booking: Village Hotel Sentosa on Booking.com
